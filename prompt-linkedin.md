**What this does:** Rewrites your entire LinkedIn profile — headline, About section, experience descriptions, skills section framing, and additional sections — to be immediately readable and attractive to  tech recruiters and hiring managers.

**How to use it:** Upload your resume and a PDF export of your LinkedIn profile to Claude, fill in the bracketed fields below, then run the prompt.

> **How to export your LinkedIn profile as PDF:** Go to your LinkedIn profile → click “More” → “Save to PDF”. Upload that file alongside your resume.
> 

---

## The prompt

```
# LinkedIn Profile Rewrite Prompt

I'm a software engineer targeting tech roles. Please rewrite my LinkedIn profile to be clear, specific, and attractive to tech recruiters and hiring managers.

I've uploaded my resume and a PDF of my current LinkedIn profile.

Target role level: [senior / staff / lead / EM]
Target company stage: [seed / Series A / Series B / any]
Specialty: [backend / full-stack / infrastructure / data / ML / mobile]

Please rewrite:

1. Headline — use this structure: [What I build] + [for whom] + [the thing that makes me specific]. Max 220 characters. Make it sound like a person, not a job posting.

2. About section — three paragraphs: (1) what I do and for whom, specific and direct; (2) what I've built that's worth knowing about, with real stakes; (3) what I'm looking for and how to reach me. First person throughout. No buzzwords.

3. Experience bullets — rewrite the top 2–3 bullets for each role using health tech framing: domain context, stakes, compliance frameworks named, and the before/after story where possible.

4. Skills section — suggest the top 15 skills I should list, prioritized by what health tech recruiters and ATS systems are actually searching for.

5. Additional sections — recommend any section I should add based on my background (volunteer work, open source, publications, speaking, etc.) and draft what to write in it.

After the rewrite, give me a short list of the three highest-impact changes you made and why.
```
